Markup language

Markup language performed combining of the text with some additional information related to the text.
Additional information, such as the structure of the text and the way of its presentation, is expressed using markers that are inserted in the primary text. The best known language for making into modern use is HTML(HyperText Markup Language),Historically marking has been used(and is still used) in publishing in the process of communication between authors, publishers and printers. Languages for making ore often divided into three classes:presenatational, procedural and descriptive.
